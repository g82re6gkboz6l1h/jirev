# The Future of Web Development in 2025 and Beyond

Web development is evolving faster than ever, driven by performance demands and better developer experience.

## Key Trends

- Server Components and Edge Rendering
- TypeScript as the default standard
- Headless CMS architectures
- WebAssembly (WASM)

## Developer Experience

Frameworks like Next.js, Astro, and Remix are simplifying complex workflows while improving performance.

## Final Words

The future belongs to developers who stay adaptable and continuously learn new technologies.
